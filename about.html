The Chief

Michael Scelfo

Michae's passion for the culinary arts was born in family kitchens, where he absorbed all he could about his heritage through food and cooking.

He was always enamored with the energy and bustle in those kitchens as everyone lent hands to make even the most modest of weekend lunches feel like memorable feasts.

His first cooking jobs were all centered around pizza and his deep love for the dish has only grown throughout the years. Michael, known for his American and Italian fare, owns internationally acclaimed restaurants, Alden & Harlow, Waypoint and The Longfellow Bar, where he has extended the concept of the domestic kitchen table.
